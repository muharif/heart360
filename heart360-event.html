About the Event

Heart 360¬∞ is a focused seminar exploring the transformative role of artificial intelligence (AI) across the cardiovascular disease spectrum, from early discovery to prevention. As cardiovascular disease remains a leading global health burden, the integration of AI into research, clinical care, drug development, and public health offers new opportunities to enhance understanding, precision, and outcomes.

The seminar is structured into four sessions: Discovery, Diagnosis, Drug Development, and Prevention, bringing together experts from academia, healthcare, and industry. Each session will highlight how AI-driven tools are reshaping translational research, enabling earlier and more accurate diagnoses, accelerating therapeutic innovation, and supporting data-driven prevention strategies.

By fostering interdisciplinary dialogue, Heart 360¬∞ aims to showcase cutting-edge applications of AI, identify key challenges, and inspire collaborative efforts to drive the future of cardiovascular science and care.
